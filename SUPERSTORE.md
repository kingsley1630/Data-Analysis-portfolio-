## ✅ RECOMENDATION

Stop discounting Tables and Supplies. These sub-categories are losing money at scale. Either reprice them, reduce discounts, or consider discontinuing the lowest performers.

Investigate Texas, Illinois, and Ohio. They generate significant sales revenue but are consistently loss-making — suggesting deep discounting, high return rates, or unprofitable customer segments in those states.

Invest more in Technology. It has the highest margin at 18% and the highest absolute profit. Prioritising this category in marketing and inventory will grow profits faster than pushing Furniture.

Capitalize on Q4 seasonality. Sales nearly triple in Sep–Dec. Ensure stock levels, staffing, and logistics are scaled ahead of September to capture that demand fully.

Examine Furniture strategy. At only 1% margin, almost any disruption (returns, discounts, shipping costs) pushes it into loss territory. Consider a premium-only approach or exiting low-margin furniture lines.
